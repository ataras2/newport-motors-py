To get the newport motor to be correctly detected as FTDI, do the following
(taken from https://raspberrypi.stackexchange.com/a/91285)

sudo gedit /sys/bus/usb-serial/drivers/ftdi_sio/new_id

add in "<idVendor> <idProduct>"

notes about automation: https://unix.stackexchange.com/questions/67936/attaching-usb-serial-device-with-custom-pid-to-ttyusb0-on-embedded/165845
